# **App Name**: CVForge

## Core Features:

- Guided Information Entry: Users are guided through a step-by-step process to input contact details, work history, education, and skills into structured sections.
- Template Selection: Users can browse and select from a gallery of modern, professional resume templates.
- Live Resume Preview: Provide a real-time preview of the resume as information is entered and templates are selected, reflecting changes instantly.
- AI-Powered Bullet Point Generator: A tool that helps users craft impactful bullet points for work experience by suggesting improvements or new phrasing based on keywords and job responsibilities.
- Skills and Education Management: Users can easily add, edit, and categorize skills and education entries, including degrees, institutions, and dates.
- Resume Export to PDF: Enables users to download their final resume as a high-quality, professional PDF document, ready for application.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A clear, professional blue (#3B7BF0) to evoke trust and clarity. Chosen for its balanced saturation and brightness against a light background.
- Background color: A subtle, desaturated off-white with a hint of blue (#F8FAFB) for a clean and spacious canvas, ensuring excellent readability.
- Accent color: A crisp, slightly brighter turquoise (#38C3DB) for interactive elements and highlights, analogous to the primary blue but with distinct contrast in brightness and saturation.
- Headline and Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, neutral, and highly readable qualities across various text sizes, ensuring a professional presentation.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, line-based icons for navigation, actions, and content categorization, maintaining a unified and modern aesthetic.
- Prioritize a grid-based, minimalist layout with ample whitespace to ensure focus on content, clear hierarchy, and easy navigation between sections.
- Incorporate subtle and functional micro-animations for transitions between steps, form submissions, and template previews, enhancing user experience without distraction.
